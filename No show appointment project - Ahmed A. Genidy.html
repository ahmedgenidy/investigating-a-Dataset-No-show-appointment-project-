Introduction¶

Welcome to the No Show Appointments Project! in this project we have a dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. In particular, We will be interested to find trends among patients who show up for thier appointment or not. and how some features like: Neighbourhood, Scholarship, Hipertension, Diabetes, Alcoholism, Handcap and SMS_received affects the patient to show up for their appointments or not.

the important question is: What factors are important for us to know in order to predict if a patient will show up for their scheduled appointment?

Data Cleaning¶

After Loading the data, we noted that:

  • the data consists of (110527 rows * 14 columns)
  • The Age varies between -1 to 115.
  • Scholarship,Hipertension, Diabetes and Alcoholism data are 0 or 1.
  • Handcap varies between 1 to 4
  • ScheduledDay and AppointmentDay are stored as string not date.
  • No-show data is stored as sring
  • There is no NUL data
  • There is no duplicated rows.

So We will do some cleaning for our data as below:

Conclusions¶

With the given data set, We can coclude that:

  • Age, Hipertension, Diabetes, Alcoholis, Handcap and SMS_received, Gender and Neighbourhood affects the patiants to show for the appointments.

The data has some limitation such as:

  • The data for Females are more than Males. this will affect the accuracy of the analysis
  • The data for some Neighbourhood are more than others. this will affect the accuracy of the analysis
  • There is one value with Age is less than Zero. Mabye
  • The handcap inputs also have data more than 1. Maybe is this typo.
